Frequently Asked Questions

My alarm didn't go off — what happened? ▾
iOS requires certain permissions for alarms to fire reliably. Make sure notifications are enabled for Forge in Settings → Notifications → Forge. Also check that your device is not in Focus/Do Not Disturb mode, which can suppress alerts. If your device volume is muted via the hardware switch, alarms may not produce sound.
How do I preview alarm sounds before setting them? ▾
When creating or editing an alarm, tap the sound name to open the sound picker. Tap any sound in the list to hear a preview. Once you find the one you want, select it and save your alarm.
Can I set different sounds for different alarms? ▾
Yes. Each alarm you create can have its own custom sound. You can have a Forge alarm for weekdays and a Storm alarm for weekends — totally independent of each other.
Does Forge work when my phone is on silent? ▾
On iOS, the hardware silent switch affects alarm behavior. For the most reliable alarm delivery, we recommend keeping your device off silent mode when alarms are set. iOS native alarms bypass the silent switch in some cases — Forge alarms follow iOS notification guidelines and behavior may vary by iOS version.
Is Forge free? Are there any in-app purchases? ▾
Forge is completely free to download and use. There are no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no ads. All sounds and features are included at no cost.
How do I delete an alarm? ▾
Swipe left on any alarm in the alarm list to reveal the delete option, then tap Delete. Your alarm will be removed immediately.
I found a bug — how do I report it? ▾
We'd love to hear about it. Email us at support@forgeapp.io with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what happened. Screenshots or screen recordings are always helpful and get your issue resolved faster.
What iOS versions does Forge support? ▾
Forge requires iOS 15.0 or later. For the best experience, we recommend keeping your device updated to the latest iOS version.
