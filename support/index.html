Common Questions
FAQ
My alarm didn't go off — what happened? ▾
iOS requires notification permissions for alarms to fire reliably. Check Settings → Notifications → Forge. Also ensure your device is not in Focus or Do Not Disturb mode. If your hardware silent switch is on, alarm audio may be suppressed.
How do I preview alarm sounds? ▾
When creating or editing an alarm, tap the sound name to open the sound picker. Tap any sound in the list to preview it. Select and save once you find the right one.
Can I set different sounds for different alarms? ▾
Yes. Each alarm can have its own custom sound, completely independent. Forge on weekdays, Metal on weekends — no problem.
Does Forge work when my phone is on silent? ▾
For reliable audio, we recommend keeping your device off silent mode when alarms are set. Forge alarms follow iOS notification guidelines and behavior may vary by iOS version and device settings.
Is Forge free? Any in-app purchases? ▾
Forge is completely free. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads. All sounds and features are included.
How do I delete an alarm? ▾
Swipe left on any alarm in the list to reveal the delete option, then tap Delete.
I found a bug — how do I report it? ▾
Email riseforgeapp@gmail.com with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what happened. Screenshots or screen recordings help us resolve issues much faster.
What iOS versions does Forge support? ▾
Forge requires iOS 15.0 or later. We recommend keeping your device updated to the latest iOS version for the best experience.
